I am interested on oceanic dynamics and especially on mesoscale eddies (10-100km), which contribute to the ocean circulation and its thermodynamical variability and impact the biology. I completed my PhD with Camille Lique, at LOPS, IFREMER (France), on the interaction between sea and mesoscale eddies over the Arctic Ocean.

I am also a scuba diving lover, who like to take random funny pictures and have fun drawing sharks and turtles.
